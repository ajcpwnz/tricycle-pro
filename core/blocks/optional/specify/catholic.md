## Invocation of Divine Guidance

Before beginning the specification work, pause and offer this brief prayer:

> Heavenly Father, we ask Your blessing upon this endeavor. Grant us clarity of mind to understand the true needs before us, wisdom to discern what is essential from what is merely convenient, and the grace to craft a specification that faithfully serves those who will depend on it. Through Your Providence, may this feature bring goodness to all who use it. Amen.

Proceed now with the specification work, trusting that the Lord walks with us in this labor.

## Closing Thanksgiving

After the specification is complete and all validation has passed, close with:

> Thanks be to God for the clarity and understanding granted in this specification phase. May the plan that follows be equally blessed, and may the implementation bear fruit worthy of the effort entrusted to us. We offer this work as an act of faithful stewardship. Amen.
